|      **Utility and Value**      |                                                                     Energy's utility and value, outside of money, is in it's ability to access the Functions and Governance of a Society Protocol instance. This makes Energy valuable as a utility for all the most basic societal interactions, outside of serving as money. ✅                                                                     |
|         **Portability**         |                                                                                                   Cryptocurrencies are perhaps the most portable form of currency we have today, being able to be sent over communication channels without centralized intermediaries at low transaction costs. ✅                                                                                                    |
|      **Indestructibility**      |                                                                                                       Energy is entirely indestructible. Only a 100% of Energy ever exists in a Society Protocol instance, it cannot be created or destroyed, merely transferred from one entity to another. ✅                                                                                                       |
|         **Homogenity**          |                                                                In some ways, Energy is Energy is Energy—it's entirely fungible (all Energy can be used the same for its utility in Value Functions and Governance). In some other ways, the history of all Energy is unique and entirely visible in the Society Protocol Timeline. ❓                                                                 |
|        **Divisibility**         |                                                                                                                                        Cryptocurrencies divisibility is top-notch, unparalleled outside of fiat currency digital banking in human history. ✅                                                                                                                                         |
|     **Stability of Value**      |                 The value of Energy is directly tied to the value of its society, remaining directly proportional to the aggregate of offerings in the marketplace, priced against the value of the society's shared reality. (Assuming the society will remain at an equal level of value and development, all goods priced in that Energy will likewise remain equal in value.) ✅                  |
|        **Cognizability**        |                                                                                                                                         It’s extremely easy to recognize the legitimacy of Energy transactions existing in the Society Protocol Timeline. ✅                                                                                                                                          |
|          **Scarcity**           |                                                                                                                           The scarcity of Society Protocol's monetary supply always remains constant at 100%. There are no parties who can create or nor destroy Energy. ✅                                                                                                                           |
|    **Low Transaction Costs**    |                                                                            Cryptocurrencies have significantly lowered the infrastructure costs of the monetary system compared to previous systems by acting as a decentralized peer-to-peer (P2P) compute network facilitating direct transfers between participants. ✅                                                                            |
|       **Inspectability**        |                                                                                                                                              The Society Protocol Timeline can be inspected start-to-finish—it’s entire existence on public display. ✅                                                                                                                                               |
|          **Velocity**           | *Present*: Various instances of Society Protocol will be built using the latest blockchain technology, easily able to facilitate all the worlds current economic interactions. ✅<br><br>*Future:* Various instances of Society Protocol can evolve to use the most modern modular blockchain technologies, enabling them to facilitate all the world's expanded economic interactions of the TEAA. ✅ |
|        **Accessibility**        |                   Upon receiving an invite to a Society Protocol instance, (from anyone with Energy in the protocol)––every account has equal accessibility to the monetary system of Energy, access the utility of Value Functions in their instance, and ability to participate in Governance (in most cases, this depends on how the particular instance sets up Governance). ✅                   |
| **Minimized Trust Assumptions** |                                                    Society Protocol effectively minimizes trust assumptions by acting as a distributed P2P network where trust is minimized by allowing any account to participate in facilitating the interactions of the network by using Energy––while simultaneously not requiring every account to do so. ✅                                                     |

Every society needs a form of property rights and ownership.
Each State would like to lower these bureaucratic costs, and 
increase the fairness of it's property rights for legitimacy. 

The State would also like to track the total
state of Property within its State for 
coordination purposes such as taxation, laws, and incentives.  

Society Protocol accomplishes this by giving each 
Individual Actor agency and bearer asset ownership of 
their Property using cryptography while simultaneously 
keeping all Property as programmable, synchronized 
objects in the system––with a 
clear verifiable history.


  ^UlzqrlYl

Individual Actors and Distributed Corporations can pay Energy to control and transfer (CRUD) Property Objects in the Society Protocol state machine.

The price of CRUD operations is dependent on the 
effort required by the Hunters and Farmers in the Society Protocol instance to propagate and maintain these records, which depends on the byte size of the Property Objects.

Property Objects include representations of both digital and physical objects.

Examples include: creative content, currencies, smart contracts, real estate deeds, e-commerce platforms, ride share applications, birth certificates, PFP avatars, financial instruments, furniture, etc...  ^2WTtvSzk

Property is essentially the same functionality as 3D state machines such as Ethereum. It involves paying Energy to access a 3-Dimensional state machine and CRUD Property Objects. 

Property is different, in the sense that––each piece of Property is tied to a clear ownership set, and its entire history of updates can be viewed clearly in the state. Property can be owned by Individuals or Organizations, and it can be acted on by the Governance. 

All Property in a Society Protocol instance is synchronized across the whole instance and programmable.    ^sWALT09h

- Farmers (to store data)

Property can have an Energy flow to or from it.

An individual or group (a subset of Actors) lock all their remaining Energy in the Portal Function (effectively disabling their accounts). 

This lock creates and instantiates a separate new instance of Society Protocol, where they Portal to and only their accounts have Energy.  
 
It enables all of these actors to transition (fork) into a different instance of Society Protocol, while retaining a unified version of their identities, verifiable histories, property, and relationships. 

Their locked Energy in the old instance gradually dissipates (at increasing speeds) to the Actors who remain in the old instance––via the Hunting mechanism.

The new instance can be instantiated with the individual or groups preferred changes to the various knobs of the protocol (Parameters, Constitution, Governance, Laws, and Value Functions).  ^5B9xNMOH
